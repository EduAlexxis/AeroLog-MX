Privacy Policy for AeroLog

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

1 Introduction

AeroLog ("we," "our," or "the App") is a digital maintenance logbook application developed by Eduardo Flamenco. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data you generate while using our application.

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled, stored, and processed. By using AeroLog, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2 Data Collection and Storage

No Developer Access to Data

We utilize a "zero-knowledge" architecture. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, inventory logs, or photos to our own servers. The developer (Eduardo Flamenco) has no way to access, view, or mine the data you enter into the App.

iCloud and CloudKit

AeroLog uses Apple's CloudKit service to synchronize your data across your devices.

  • Private Database: All inventory data, part numbers, locations, and images are stored in a private iCloud container (iCloud.com.EduAlexxiss.AeroLog) associated with your personal Apple ID.
  • Control: This data is encrypted by Apple and is only accessible by you on devices signed in to your iCloud account. We cannot view or decrypt this data.

Local Storage

If you do not have iCloud enabled, all data is stored locally on your device's internal storage using the SwiftData framework.

3 Camera Usage and Image Processing

AeroLog requests access to your device's camera for two specific purposes:

  • Intelligent Scanner: To scan part tags and extract text (Part Numbers) using Optical Character Recognition (OCR).
  • Visual Evidence: To capture photos of parts and tags for your maintenance records.

On-Device Processing

  • OCR Scanning: All text recognition is performed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Video frames from the scanner are processed in real-time memory and are never uploaded to any external server.
  • Photo Storage: Photos you choose to save are stored in your local database or your private iCloud container. They are never shared with third parties or the developer.

4 Analytics and Tracking

AeroLog is a professional utility tool designed for privacy.

  • No Third-Party Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, or Mixpanel) to track user behavior.
  • No Advertising: The App does not display advertisements and does not share data with advertising networks.

5 Data Retention

Because your data is stored in your personal iCloud account or local device storage, you retain full control over its retention.

  • Deletion: If you delete a part entry within the App, it is removed from your local database and your iCloud Private Database immediately.
  • App Deletion: If you delete the AeroLog app, local data is removed. Data synced to iCloud may remain in your iCloud storage until you manually delete the app data via your iOS Settings (Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Account Storage).
