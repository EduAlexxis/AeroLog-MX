Part I: Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy.

1. Introduction

AeroLog ("we," "our," or "the App") is a digital maintenance logbook application developed by Eduardo Flamenco. We utilize a "zero-knowledge" architecture: we do not collect, store, or mine your personal data on our own servers.

2. Data Storage & Platform Differences

AeroLog stores data locally on your device, with platform-specific sync options:

  • iOS/iPadOS: Data is stored using SwiftData. If enabled, data syncs to your personal, encrypted iCloud container. We have no access to this container.
  • Android: Data is stored locally in an encrypted database. There is no automatic cloud synchronization. Data remains on the device unless manually exported by the user.

3. Camera Usage

We use the camera for the Intelligent Scanner (OCR) and for attaching photos to parts. All image processing happens locally on your device. No video or images are sent to external servers for analysis.

4. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) and we do not display ads. We respect your "Do Not Track" settings.

5. Data Portability (Import/Export)

The Import/Export feature allows you to create manual backups of your database. These files are stored in your device's file system or a location of your choosing. You are solely responsible for the security and storage of these exported files.
