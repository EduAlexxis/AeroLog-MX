Changelog

New updates and improvements to AeroLog.

Version 1.1

Compatibility Update
December 18, 2025

We've expanded accessibility to more devices by optimizing our core systems.

  • Legacy Support: AeroLog now supports devices running iOS 17 and iPadOS 17 or later.
  • Performance Tweaks: Improved UI responsiveness on older hardware.

Version 1.0

Initial Release
December 5, 2025

Welcome to AeroLog! Our first release brings professional-grade maintenance logging to both iOS and Android.

  • Cross-Platform: Available now for iPhone, iPad, and Android devices.
  • Smart Scanner: Scan Part Numbers directly from tags using the camera.
  • iCloud Sync (iOS): Automatic private synchronization between Apple devices.
  • Data Portability (Android Exclusive): Built-in Import/Export tools to manage your database manually and keep your data backed up.
  • Blueprint UI: Dark-mode optimized engineering aesthetic.
  • Evidence: Attach "Tag" and "Part" photos to every entry.
