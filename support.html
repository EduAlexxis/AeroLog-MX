Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device or in your personal private iCloud. The developer cannot access your data.

Does it work offline?

Absolutely. You can log parts and take photos without internet. Data will sync to iCloud automatically once you are back online.
